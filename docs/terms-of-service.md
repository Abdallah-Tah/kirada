# How It Works

**Last updated:** June 30, 2026

## What is Kirada?

Kirada is a property management app that helps landlords, tenants, and maintenance teams stay organized. Track properties, manage leases, record rent payments, handle maintenance requests, sign contracts digitally, and communicate — all in one place.

**Kirada does not handle money.** It tracks invoice status and payment records, but all actual payments happen outside the platform through whatever method you agree on (bank transfer, cash, check, etc.).

---

## For Landlords

### Getting Started
1. **Register** as a landlord at [kirada.buildwithabdallah.com](https://kirada.buildwithabdallah.com)
2. **Add properties** — Create buildings, units, and track occupancy
3. **Invite tenants** — Send invitations; tenants create their own accounts
4. **Create leases** — Set lease terms, rent amounts, dates, and deposits
5. **Generate invoices** — Create rent invoices; tenants see them and record payments
6. **Manage maintenance** — Tenants submit requests; you assign them to maintenance workers
7. **Sign contracts** — Create digital contracts and send signing links to tenants

### Key Features
- **Property dashboard:** Vacancy rates, occupied units, rent status at a glance
- **Tenant management:** Profiles, lease history, contact info
- **Lease tracking:** Start/end dates, renewal reminders, deposit records
- **Invoice system:** Generate invoices, mark as paid/unpaid, upload payment proof
- **Maintenance workflow:** Request → Assign → Track → Resolve
- **Digital contracts:** Create, send, e-sign, and archive as PDF
- **Messaging:** Communicate with tenants directly in the app
- **Documents:** Store and share important files securely
- **AI assistant:** Ask property management questions, get suggestions

---

## For Tenants

### Getting Started
1. **Accept invitation** — Your landlord sends an email invitation
2. **Create account** — Set your password and complete your profile
3. **View your lease** — See lease terms, rent amount, and dates
4. **Track invoices** — View rent invoices and mark payments
5. **Submit maintenance requests** — Report issues with photos and descriptions
6. **Sign contracts** — Review and e-sign contracts sent by your landlord
7. **Message your landlord** — Communicate directly through the app

### Key Features
- **Lease dashboard:** Your lease terms, rent status, and important dates
- **Invoice view:** See what's due, what's paid, and download records
- **Maintenance requests:** Submit issues, attach photos, track progress
- **Contract signing:** Review and sign leases and agreements digitally
- **Messaging:** Talk to your landlord without sharing personal contact info
- **Multi-language:** Switch the interface language anytime

---

## For Maintenance Workers

### Getting Started
1. **Receive assignment** — Landlord assigns you a maintenance request
2. **Review details** — See the issue description, photos, and unit location
3. **Update status** — Mark progress as you work
4. **Complete and report** — Mark resolved and add notes about the fix

---

## How Rent Tracking Works

```
Landlord creates invoice  →  Tenant sees invoice  →  Tenant pays outside Kirada
        ↓                           ↓                         ↓
   Due date set            Payment recorded         Status marked "Paid"
   Amount set              (by tenant or              Proof uploaded
                            landlord)
```

**Kirada tracks the status — it does not move money.** You and your tenant decide how to actually pay (bank, cash, check, etc.). Kirada just keeps everyone on the same page.

---

## How Maintenance Requests Work

```
Tenant reports issue  →  Landlord reviews  →  Assigned to maintenance
      ↓                      ↓                      ↓
  Photo + description   Priority + notes      Worker updates status
                                                     ↓
                                              Marked "Resolved"
```

---

## How Digital Contracts Work

```
Landlord creates contract  →  System generates PDF  →  Signing link sent to tenant
         ↓                         ↓                          ↓
   Terms written            Stored securely            Tenant reviews & signs
                                                              ↓
                                                       Signed PDF archived
```

Electronic signatures may carry legal validity depending on your jurisdiction. For important agreements, consult a qualified attorney.
